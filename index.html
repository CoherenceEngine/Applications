Core Method

From raw signal to operational action

01 Receive signals

Ingest time-series, system, physiological, device, machine, or process data from existing environments.

02 Transform structure

03 Compute invariant

Generate a protected coherence-based metric that becomes the basis for interpretation.

04 Classify system state
