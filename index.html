Licensing Pathways

Three ways partners can license the IP

Not every partner should enter the platform the same way. This model gives the company a clear way to structure early access, recurring licensing, and larger strategic relationships.

Pilot License

A limited-scope license designed for early deployment, validation, or commercial testing in a defined domain or operating environment.

Best for: Design partners, early operators, innovation teams, and first sector adopters

Primary value: Creates proof, lowers adoption friction, and opens the door to recurring revenue

Annual Commercial License

A recurring licensing model tied to software use, workflow deployment, infrastructure monitoring, or domain-specific commercial implementation.

Best for: Enterprise platforms, operational software environments, and recurring-use partners

Primary value: Transforms the IP from project-based opportunity into recurring revenue

Strategic or Embedded License

A broader licensing structure for integration into larger systems, product ecosystems, or multi-domain commercial relationships.

Best for: Major platforms, infrastructure partners, strategic investors, and long-term commercial collaborators

Primary value: Creates expansion potential, broader market access, and larger enterprise value
