Investor FAQ

Key questions investors and partners will ask

What exactly is the core IP?
The core IP is a coherence-based computation and control framework that includes a field equation, coherence metric, transformation pipeline, and control logic for measuring and acting on system behavior.
Is this a single-product company or a platform IP company?
It is best framed as a platform IP company with the ability to support multiple operating products, partners, and deployment layers.
Why is the licensing opportunity large?
Because the same protected architecture can be deployed across multiple technical sectors rather than being limited to one narrow product category.
What is the most practical first commercialization path?
The most practical first path is licensing or co-developing with partners who already control data, hardware, infrastructure, or operational environments.
How do you validate technical credibility?
The portfolio supports structured testing, measurement, and comparison against baselines, which is critical for diligence, pilots, and technical partnerships.
What makes this investable before full market penetration?
The value comes from patent ownership, multiple monetization paths, broad application coverage, and the ability to create both licensing revenue and downstream product upside.
